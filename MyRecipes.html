Lavender Veil
  • 5ml fixative
  • 8ml Lavender essential oil
  • 6ml Bergamot essential oil
  • 3ml Sandalwood essential oil
  1. Pour fixative into a clear glass bottle
  2. Add in all the essential oils.
  3. Close the bottle and swirl gently for 30 seconds.
  4. Let the scent rest for 48–72 hours before use.

Warm Amber
  • 6ml Amber
  • 4ml Sandalwood
  • 3ml Taif Rose
  • 3ml Bergamot
  1. Add all fragrance ingredients into a glass bottle.
  2. Gently swirl (do not shake aggressively).
  3. Let the perfume mature for 7–10 days for best depth.

Sillage Perfumes

A scent recipe sharing platform for people who want a cleaner, more intentional approach to fragrance. It exists for those who are mindful of what they put on their bodies and are seeking alternatives to commercial perfumes that can contain undisclosed or harmful ingredients.

Sillage is French for the trace a scent leaves behind, a reminder that even consciously crafted fragrances can be just as powerful and memorable.
