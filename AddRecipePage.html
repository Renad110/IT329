Sillage Perfumes

A scent recipe sharing platform for people who want a cleaner, more intentional approach to fragrance. It exists for those who are mindful of what they put on their bodies and are seeking alternatives to commercial perfumes that can contain undisclosed or harmful ingredients.

Sillage is French for the trace a scent leaves behind, a reminder that even consciously crafted fragrances can be just as powerful and memorable.
